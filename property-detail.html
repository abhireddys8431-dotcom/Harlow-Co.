The Story

Carved into the rugged Alpine contours of Aspen, Colorado, The Obsidian Villa represents a pinnacle of luxury chalet architecture. Meticulously framed in blackened iron, local cedar, and massive slabs of natural volcanic stone, this breathtaking property strikes a spectacular silhouette against the snow-capped Rockies.

The residence is designed as an architectural dialog between grand entertaining spaces and private wellness sanctuaries. Vaulted double-height ceilings span the main salon, where a hand-crafted basalt stone hearth serves as a majestic focal point. Floor-to-ceiling sliding glass facade walls glide back effortlessly to merge the interior living spaces with a massive thermal patio.

No detail has been spared in the home’s layout. Every finish, from the European green oak cabinetry to the brushed champagne brass accents, has been carefully selected to complement the spectacular forest backdrop.
